About Stacy

Three decades leading high-performance teams — now helping you build yours.

I spent my career delivering results in complex organizations. Now I help leaders deliver results through people — without carrying it all themselves.

I'm Stacy Balzer, a former Senior Director at Ford Motor Company and founder of Uplift+Ignite Coaching and Consulting. For 30+ years, I led large, complex organizations supporting critical operations, developing leaders, and driving performance at scale. I was often brought in to fix what wasn't working.

And what I learned is this: the real lever isn't just process. It's people.

I've led cross-functional teams across operations, strategy, and customer experience through high-pressure environments and constant change. But the results I'm most proud of aren't just metrics. They're the leaders who grew. The teams that stepped up. The cultures that shifted.

Most leaders don't struggle because they lack capability. They struggle because they're carrying too much — and their teams aren't fully stepping up yet.

Today, I partner with leaders and organizations to:

  • ✓ Build confident, accountable leaders
  • ✓ Strengthen team communication and trust
  • ✓ Improve execution without burnout
  • ✓ Create cultures where people and performance thrive

My work sits at the intersection of results and people — because you shouldn't have to choose.

Encouragement isn't soft. It's a leadership discipline.

If you're ready to stop carrying it all and start building a team that performs — let's get to work.
