About Stacy

Three decades turning around service organizations — now turning my attention to you.

I spent my career fixing the things other leaders couldn't. Now I help leaders fix the things holding their teams back.

I'm Stacy Balzer, a global automotive industry executive turned coach and consultant. For thirty years at Ford Motor Company, I led service operations turnarounds — taking on the critical support organizations, systems, and processes that were stuck, and bringing them into high performance.

As Operating Director of Service Engineering Operations, I led 600+ U.S. employees and 500+ global functional teammates across Recall, Warranty, Technical Support, Technical Training, Diagnostics, and Vehicle Health. I pioneered Ford's Voice of Customer strategy and led the service conversion for connected, electric, and autonomous vehicles.

But the work I'm proudest of isn't a program or a platform — it's the people. Servant leadership, high-performance team collaboration, and devotion to people growth are what built every one of those results. That's the work I want to do with you.
